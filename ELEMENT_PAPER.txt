Revised position: Catalytic metals may enable 40 Hz through impedance properties
(facilitating multi-domain coupling), but they don‚Äôt generate it. The 40 Hz gamma
rhythm likely emerges from network dynamics, not atomic vibrations.

SH groups), not impedance mimicry.
Predicting this requires additional factors: Redox potential, electronegativity, orbital
overlap, ligand field effects.
